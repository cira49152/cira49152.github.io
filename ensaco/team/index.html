Our Team

We help you sync with Industry 4.0 in motion!

ENSACO Solutions team jointly have more than 75 years of experience in managing, developing solutions and commissioning in fields of Oil & Gas industry and Power Industry.

Želimir Lukač

Želimir Lukač is electrical engineer with more than 35 years of experience in design and commissioning of systems automation and supervision in Power, Oil & Gas, chemical and rubber Industry, transport systems etc. He worked as team leader of large integral automation projects, deployed in design, development, commissioning of PLC / SCADA / MES system applications and engineering, implementation, testing, commissioning in Serbia, Czech Republic, Russia, Belorussia. He is responsible designer (license: 350 and 352) and responsible supervisor (License: 450).

Vladan Cvejić

Vladan Cvejić is power engineer with more than 20 years of experience in field of Transmission and distribution of electrical energy. He did engineering, implementation, testing, commissioning, automation systems standardization (Power Automation Control) in Transmission and distribution system worldwide. He is member of: IEC technical committee TC57 (Working group 10 – control and communication in Power systems, Working group 17 - Power system intelligent electronic device communication and associated data models for microgrids, distributed energy resources and distribution automation), IEC technical committee TC95 (Working group 2 - Protection functions with Digital Input / Output), Institute for standardization of Serbia, CIGRE Serbia. He is responsible designer (license: 351) and responsible supervisor (license: 451).

Dušan Ćirić

Dušan Ćirić is highly-experienced C/C++ programmer, with 20 years of experience, highly capable of solving and designing large scale projects. He engineered, designed and implemented new generation of general-purpose SCADA system (InfoControl SCADA), installed in many industries across the Serbia, Croatia, Macedonia, Montenegro, Romania, Russia and Belorussia. He designed, developed and implemented MES system in RNP (Oil&Gas Company of Serbia). He also developed and implemented communication stacks of following protocols: SNTP, MMS 2.0 (Client and Server side), GOOSE (according to IEC 61850 standard (Ed.1 and Ed.2)), Modbus, DNP3, OPC UA.
